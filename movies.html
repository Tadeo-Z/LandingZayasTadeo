Here I will show some of my favorite movies & series

I really struggle to watch movies or TV shows. I mean, it's rare that I just say to myself, "I'm going to watch this movie/TV show," even when people recommend something to me, it usually takes me a long time to get around to watching it. Even so, I've obviously seen a lot of TV shows and movies, you can see my favorites below.

  • Marriage story

    • I watched this movie in 2020 while I was on vacation about to enter college. Due to problems at home and in my personal life, this movie, despite being quite raw, really helped me out when I watched it. I even learned a little from it.

  • 47 Ronin

    • While this movie adds fantastical elements and things that didn't happen to a real event, I love the plot, I love the pace of the movie, and I love Keanu Reeves' acting as a "half-breed Samurai." It may not be the best movie in the world, but it's special to me.

  • Now you see me

    • This movie has a particular detail about it. I know that the plot doesn't make any sense and is often inconsistent, but despite that, I can't help but like it so much and from time to time I will watch it again.

  • Pacific Rim

    • I remember seeing this movie when it came out in theaters. I was so excited because it was about giant robots fighting monsters that came out of the ocean. I was probably too young to understand the weight of the plot, but as the years went by, I stopped liking it just for being a "Monsters vs. Robots" movie and started to like it more for the plot of the main characters.

  • Breaking bad

    • I only started watching this series last year, and I haven't even finished it yet, but it's already my absolute favorite without a doubt. The plot is excellent, the characters are well-written, and the actors are fantastic in their roles. I think it's a gem of a series, no matter how you look at it.

  • Squid game

    • I watched this series a few months after it came out, I remember not being able to stop watching it the moment I started. I watched all nine episodes in just two days, which is a record considering that when I watch a series, I usually only watch one or two episodes a day. Since I finished it, I've been eagerly awaiting the second season, but the series director said it would take as long as it needed. I'm not complaining as long as a second season comes out with the same or better quality than the first.

  • Lupin

    • I started watching this series with a friend one time when we had a sleepover at another friend's house in the same group. The series is about a very intelligent French thief who is also an excellent gentleman. The plot has twists, unexpected elements, and even cliffhangers. I highly recommend it - it's very entertaining and great for having a good time watching something.

  • Arcane

    • Arcane, a series created by Riot Games in 2021. I remember not watching it until 2022, but as soon as I did, I had a similar experience to when I watched Squid Game. I was completely hooked and watched all the episodes in just two days. The script, the pace of the series, the characters, the voice actors - everything is perfect in this series. It's a series that I recommend to all my gamer friends.
